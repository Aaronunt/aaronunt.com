About

Aaron Unt (born 20 June 2006) is an Estonian tech entrepreneur from Pärnu. He is the co-founder of Valinor Partners and Jarvik Group, and a founding board member of Gen Z Founders Society, a network that connects Estonia's most promising young founders. Together with his business partner he built a direct-to-consumer e-commerce portfolio that reached over $4.6M in annual recurring revenue.

In January 2025 he attended the World Economic Forum in Davos, representing the Global Shapers community in Tallinn. He also founded Teadustäht MTÜ, a nonprofit focused on science education.

Before founding his companies, Aaron built a national profile in Estonia through science and academic competitions: he won Estonia's first Estronaut contest, competed on the science television show Rakett 69, and won the silver medal at the International Earth Science Olympiad in Beijing.
