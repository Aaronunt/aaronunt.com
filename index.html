About

Aaron Unt (born 20 June 2006) is an Estonian tech entrepreneur from Pärnu. He is the founder of Valinor Partners, co-founder of Jarvik Group OÜ, and a board member and co-founder of Gen Z Founders Society (GenZ Asutajad MTÜ), a network that connects Estonia's most promising young founders. Together with his business partner he built a direct-to-consumer e-commerce portfolio that reached over $4.6M in annual recurring revenue.

In January 2025 he attended the World Economic Forum in Davos, representing the Global Shapers community in Tallinn. He also founded Teadustäht MTÜ, a nonprofit focused on science education.

Before founding his companies, Aaron built a national profile in Estonia through science and academic competitions. He won Estonia's first Estronaut contest, competed on the science television show Rakett 69, and medaled at the International Earth Science Olympiad in Beijing. He graduated from Pärnu Koidula Gümnaasium in 2025.
